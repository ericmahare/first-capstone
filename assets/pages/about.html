    REMOVE STRAY DOGS FROM OUR STREETS BY GIVING THEM SHELTER

    Dog Vet is a multi award winning charity delivering free essential vetinary care and assistance to dogs experiencing homelessness around Kenya. Homelessness is a growing problem in the Kenya and an increasing number of homeless people have dogs. The bond between many homeless people and their dogs is profound and the health and wellbeing of their canine companions is often a major priority for their owners.

    We will use everything you give – in cash or kind – to provide much needed care for the homeless dogs across the cities in which we are active. This includes buying medications; paying for life saving operations; and equipping our team with the kit they need.
